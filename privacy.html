Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Citizenship Buddy ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

  • Audio Data: With your explicit permission, the App accesses your microphone to record your voice during interview practice sessions.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-identifiable data such as device model and OS version to improve app performance and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide voice recording and playback features for your citizenship interview practice.
  • To offer feedback on your practice sessions (if applicable).
  • To ensure technical stability and improve the user experience.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Recorded audio data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you delete the App, the locally stored data will also be removed. (Optional: If any data is processed via our FastAPI backend, it is handled securely and deleted after processing.)

4. Permissions

The App requires Microphone Access to function correctly during practice modes. You can manage or revoke this permission at any time through your device's system settings.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or audio data to outside parties.
